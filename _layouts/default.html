Jay Lee

I am a PhD student in Bernardo Sabatini lab at the Harvard Medical School.

I started the Ph.D program in Neurobiology in 2015, and joined the Sabatini lab in 2016. Before, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Bio-engineering at KAIST in 2015.

I am broadly interested in understanding circuit mechanisms of compuations in the brain, relevant for decision making. We are studying this using anatomical tracing, optogenetics, and extracellular recording.

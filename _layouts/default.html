Work during my Ph.D in Sabatini lab

Striatal D2 pathway mediate competition during lateralized action selection



Jaeeon Lee*

, Bernardo Sabatini
manuscript in prep.

We dissected the functional impact of stimulating the striatal D2 indirect pathway, on behavior and neural dynamics in a downstream region (colliculus). We show that D2 pathway can both suppress and trigger movement on the contra/ipsi side respectively. This can be explained by paradoxical excitaiton of SC contra hemisphere. We propose D2-collicular circuit motif might allow behavioral exploration.

Anatomically segregated basal ganglia pathways allow parallel behavioral modulation

Jaeeon Lee*

, Wengang Wang, Bernardo Sabatini
Nature Neuroscience, 2020,

pdf

We show topographical loops starting from striatum to Pf, VM and SC in the mouse brain. Segregated pathways in medial vs ventrolateral striatum modulate turning/licking behavior respectively, confirming parallel organization of BG output pathways.
